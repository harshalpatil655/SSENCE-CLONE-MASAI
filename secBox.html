POCKETS, PLEASE!

With Lemaire, Dries Van Noten, and Pushbutton

A pocket is a little bit like a secret. The world alone calls to mind hiding places, and the items tucked away in said hiding places. It’s not all about deception or privacy, though, but convenience, too—the unspoken luxury of carrying your items with you, on your person, wherever you go. Here, we’re turning ours inside out to show off this season’s best-kept pocket secrets.

Model wears Lemaire jacket

Lemaire Indigo Zipped Denim Jacket

The brand that arguably does modern utilitarian tailoring best—Lemaire offers its version of a jean jacket, with zippers for extra dependability.

Featured In This Image: Jil Sander wallet.

Jil Sander Black Zip Pocket Wallet

An interesting contraption featuring a collection of several pockets, some zipped, others not. A modern pocketbook, a wallet if you will.

Model wears Lemaire jacket

Maison Margiela Blue Contrast Pocket Jeans

Deconstruction king Margiela brings the inside out with these contrast pocket pants. Show off your pristine pocket lining, and keep your hands cozy too.

Featured In This Image: Smythson notebook.

Smythson Blue Soho Pocket Notebook

The easiest way to give yourself a boost of serotonin is to achieve goals. How to do this every day? By writing to-do lists and ticking them off. What better way of keeping track than this little pocketbook, in a cheerful shade of cobalt blue.

Model wears Pushbutton lounge pants.

Pushbutton Black Multi Lounge Pants

Whether you call them parachute, cargo, or tactical, they’re back—pants you can treat as a backpack. The more overfilled the pockets, the baggier the silhouette, and that’s just the way we like it.

Model wears Dries Van Noten shirt.

Dries Van Noten Navy Pocket Shirt

For a garment that tastefully celebrates the pocket, look to a member of the Antwerp Six. Dries Van Noten offers a tactically tailored pocket shirt, featuring a pocket bib applique—for versatility and mobility.
